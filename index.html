About Me

Hello! My name is Michelle Lim. I am a graduate of California State Polytechnic University, Pomona and have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I am aspiring to be a front end developer, although I do have some knowledge in back end as well. I am also interested in game and mobile development.

Fun Facts

  • I love photography! I enjoy both digital and film photography. I currently own a:
    • Canon EOS Rebel T6
    • Canon T70
    • Canon Jet135
    • Minolta Freedom Zoom 150
  • I have one cat named Tiger! He is an orange tabby and usually the main subject of my photography. He was born on April 28 in 2018.
  • In my free time I enjoy watching movies and anime. One of my favorite movies is Sympathy for Lady Vengeance (2005) and one of my favorite anime is Erased (2016).
